Faculty at RIT Dubai

Students at RIT Dubai are taught by a world-class faculty who are specialists in their fields.

Our faculty members come from diverse multicultural backgrounds with PhDs from renowned universities in the United States, Canada, UK, France, Sweden, etc.

Many of them also bring expertise from industry backgrounds to the classroom, reflecting in their innovative teaching styles and philosophies.

Besides teaching, our faculty members stay involved in ongoing research and publications within their field of instruction.
